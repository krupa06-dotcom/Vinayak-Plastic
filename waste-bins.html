Waste Bins & Dustbins

Industrial-grade waste bins for municipal, commercial, and factory use — wheeled and lidded options available.

Built for Heavy-Duty Waste Management

Our waste bins and dustbins are manufactured from UV-stabilized HDPE, designed to withstand outdoor exposure, rough handling during collection, and frequent washing. Available in capacities ranging from 50L to 1100L with wheeled and lidded configurations for municipal and commercial waste collection.

  • UV-stabilized HDPE — withstands sun exposure
  • Wheeled bins with reinforced axle (2-wheel and 4-wheel)
  • Dome lid, flat lid, and swing lid options
  • Ergonomic handles for manual tipping
  • Hot-stamping of municipal/company logos available

Applications

Municipal Collection

240L and 660L bins for door-to-door waste collection. Compatible with standard hydraulic tippers on collection trucks.

Commercial Complexes

120L and 240L bins for offices, malls, and apartment complexes. Clean design, easy to wash and maintain.

Industrial Facilities

660L and 1100L bins for factory floor waste collection. Heavy-duty casters and reinforced rim for longevity.

Public Spaces

50L and 120L bins for parks, bus stands, and public areas. UV-stabilized to prevent fading and cracking.
