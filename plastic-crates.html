Plastic Crates

Stackable & nestable crates for dairy, bakery, warehouse, and industrial material handling.

Durable, Lightweight & Stackable

Our plastic crates are manufactured using high-grade HDPE and PP materials, designed for heavy-duty material handling in dairy, bakery, cold storage, and warehouse environments. Available in multiple sizes and colors.

  • HDPE / PP grade — impact & UV resistant
  • Stackable with locking rims for safe transport
  • Nestable designs for space-saving return logistics
  • Ventilated and solid base options available
  • Custom colors and logo hot-stamping on request

Common Applications

Dairy

Milk pouch crates, curd and paneer transport crates with ventilation for moisture management.

Bakery

Bread and confectionery crates designed for stacking in delivery vehicles and storage racks.

Cold Storage

Frozen food and ice cream crates that withstand sub-zero temperatures without brittleness.

Warehouse

General material handling crates for inventory storage, order picking, and internal logistics.
