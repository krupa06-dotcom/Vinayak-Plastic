About Vinayak Plastics

Business House Engaged In Supply of All Type of Material Handling & Packaging Products.

Your Partner in
Material Handling

Vinayak Plastics is a business house engaged in the supply of all types of material handling and packaging products. We specialize in providing plastic crates, pallets, waste bins, hand pallet trucks, ice boxes, storage bins, and racking systems to warehouses, dairy distributors, municipal corporations, and commercial buyers across India.

Our product range is manufactured using HDPE and PP grades — materials chosen for impact resistance, UV stability, and long service life in demanding industrial environments. We work with buyers to deliver products that meet specific size, color, and load-bearing requirements.

Every enquiry is handled directly — no distributor layers, no automated systems. You speak with the team that understands the product.
