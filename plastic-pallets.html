Plastic Pallets

Heavy-duty pallets for warehouse racking, logistics, and export — single-face and double-face options.

Built for Heavy-Duty Logistics

Our plastic pallets are manufactured from virgin HDPE grade, designed for repeated use in warehouse racking, factory floor storage, and export shipping. They are lightweight, durable, and resistant to moisture, chemicals, and pests — a direct replacement for wooden pallets with longer service life.

  • Virgin HDPE — strong, durable, food-safe
  • Single-face (2-way entry) & double-face (4-way entry)
  • Rackable, stackable, nestable designs
  • Non-absorbent — no splinters, nails, or rot
  • ISPM-15 compliant for export (no fumigation needed)

Why Switch to Plastic Pallets?

No Splinters or Nails

Safe for manual handling and automated systems. No damaged goods from protruding nails or broken wood.

Moisture & Chemical Resistant

Impervious to water, oils, acids, and cleaning chemicals. Ideal for food processing and pharmaceutical environments.

Export Ready

ISPM-15 compliant — no heat treatment or fumigation required for international shipping. Reduces logistics overhead.

Long Service Life

3-5× longer lifespan than wooden pallets. 100% recyclable at end of life — lower total cost of ownership.
