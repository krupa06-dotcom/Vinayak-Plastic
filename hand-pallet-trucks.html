Hand Pallet Trucks

Manual pallet jacks for loading/unloading — 2.5T and 3T capacities with nylon or PU wheels.

Reliable Manual Material Handling

Our hand pallet trucks are built for daily warehouse and loading bay operations. Featuring a robust hydraulic pump unit, reinforced fork frames, and ergonomic handle controls, these trucks are designed for years of reliable service in demanding logistics environments.

  • 2.5T and 3.0T lifting capacity
  • Hydraulic pump with overload relief valve
  • Nylon wheels (standard) / PU wheels (quiet operation)
  • Fork lengths: 1000mm, 1100mm, 1200mm
  • Ergonomic handle with 3-position control (raise/neutral/lower)

Key Features

Hydraulic Pump

Sealed hydraulic unit with overload relief valve prevents damage from overloading. Easy maintenance with accessible oil fill port.

Ergonomic Handle

3-position control handle with cushioned grip. Raise, neutral, and lower positions with smooth action. Steering arc optimized for tight spaces.

Fork Frame

Reinforced steel fork frame with integrated entry/exit rollers. 200mm lift height with 80mm lowered height for easy pallet entry.

Wheel Options

Nylon wheels for rough/outdoor surfaces. PU wheels for quiet indoor operation on smooth floors. Spring-loaded rear caster for even load distribution.
